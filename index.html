F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What are motives?

Motives are the inferences from the observation of the behaviour. They are the powerful tools for the explanation of the behaviour and they allow us to make predictions about future behaviour

What is inspiration?

The process of being mentally stimulated to do or feel something, especially to do something creative.

What is unconscious motivation?

It refers to the hidden and unknown desires that are the real reasons for things that people do.

What is life ?
